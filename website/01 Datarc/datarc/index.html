About Our Product

Content Concealer is an Extension Downloadable on Google Chrome. It allows the user to block out certain words and paragraphs that can be triggering.

How It Works

Google users simply have to click the Content Concealer icon in Chrome and then enter the word that they would like to be blocked. Upon saving this word, posts and text passages containing this word will no longer be shown to the user.
